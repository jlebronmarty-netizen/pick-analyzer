# AI Goals And Readiness

Goals are conservative engineering targets, not betting guarantees.

Default targets:

- Minimum settled sample: 100
- Maximum Brier Score: 0.22
- Maximum calibration error: 8
- Minimum data sufficiency: 70
- Minimum feature quality: 70
- Minimum confidence reliability: 75
- Minimum settlement coverage: 70
- Maximum drift: 8
- Recommendation readiness target: 80

Statuses: `ACHIEVED`, `ON TRACK`, `WATCH`, `BLOCKED`, `NOT ENOUGH DATA`.

The maturity pipeline is a readiness representation only: `DATA`, `INTELLIGENCE`, `SHADOW PREDICTIONS`, `BACKTESTING`, `CALIBRATION`, `MARKET INTELLIGENCE`, `OFFICIAL ELIGIBILITY`, `PRODUCTION`.
