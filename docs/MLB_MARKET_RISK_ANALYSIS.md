# MLB Market Risk Analysis V1

Risk dimensions:
- Overfitting
- Data leakage
- Settlement ambiguity
- Small sample
- Provider dependence
- Lineup dependence
- Player availability dependence
- Late-news sensitivity
- Price volatility
- Sportsbook-rule differences
- Operational cost
- User misunderstanding

Risk grades:
- Full Game: Medium
- Team Totals: Medium
- First Five: High
- First Inning: Very High
- Pitcher Props: Very High
- Batter Props: Very High
- Team/Game Props: High
- Combined/Alternate: Very High

Key conclusion:
- Expansion should begin with Team Totals V1 as shadow-only.
- Player props should not be first despite high user value because data, identity, settlement and late-news risks are much higher.
