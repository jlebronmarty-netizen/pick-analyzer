# MLB Projection Ranking

Projection Rank Score is separate from projected value.

Components:

- Readiness.
- Confidence.
- Data sufficiency.
- Feature quality.
- Identity confidence.
- Event identity confidence.
- Starter or participation certainty.
- Historical reliability when settled projection samples exist.
- Warning penalties.
- Baseline-only penalties.

Allowed tiers:

- `ELITE`
- `STRONG`
- `MODERATE`
- `LIMITED`
- `BLOCKED`

Counting-stat rows are not ranked by the largest projected number. League-baseline-only projections cannot become top projections.
