# AI Performance Product Experience

The product experience is designed to answer:

- How Pick Analyzer is performing overall
- How each registered sport is performing
- Whether confidence can be trusted
- Which predictions were correct, incorrect, pushed or pending
- Which sports are production-ready, shadow-only or blocked

Weak grades and limited trust states are shown directly. `LIMITED` is presented as an engineering trust label, never as a betting recommendation.

Small-sample and missing-data states use plain language such as `Not enough data`.
