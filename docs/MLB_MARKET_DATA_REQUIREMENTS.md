Status labels:

- Confirmed lineups and detailed availability: Partial
- Historical odds depth: Historical-limited

- Team total line and price: Missing
- Team offense, opposing starter, bullpen, park/weather: Partial

- First-five odds: Missing
- First-five score results: Missing
- Confirmed starters: Partial
- First-five offense and starter splits: Historical-limited

First Inning:
- NRFI/YRFI odds: Missing
- First-inning results: Missing
- Top-order lineup confirmation: Partial
- Starter first-inning profile: Historical-limited

Pitcher Props:
- Verified prop odds: Provider-limited
- Confirmed starter identity: Partial
- Pitch count/rest/game-log history: Partial
- Prop settlement fields: Missing

Batter Props:
- Verified prop odds: Provider-limited
- Confirmed lineup and batting order: Partial
- Batter matchup and handedness history: Historical-limited
- Prop settlement fields: Missing

Combined/Alternate:
- Multi-book or ladder prices: Missing
- Correlation-safe line history: Missing
